What Are Volatile Organic Compounds?

Volatile Organic Compounds are organic chemicals found indoors that contain carbon. They are typically released through household products such as paints, solvents, wood preservatives, aerosol sprays and cleaning products. Many types of chemicals are classfied under 'VOCs' and although many are harmless, there are some that can cause some serious damage to your health.

Health Effects

Mild effects include:

  • Eye, nose and throat irritation
  • Headaches
  • Skin allergy reactions
  • Fatigue
  • Epistaxis

Severe effects include:

  • Loss of coordination and nausea
  • Dyspnea
  • Liver, kidney and central nervous system damage
  • Cancer

Are You Vulnerable?

Some people are more vulnerable to VOCs than others. The health effects depends on the level of concentration, type of chemicals present, length of exposure, and whether the person is asthmatic, a young child or elderly.
