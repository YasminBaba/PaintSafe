What is PaintSafe?

Overview

PaintSafe is a simple device that continuously measures the level of VOCs in your workspace. If the level become too high, PaintSafe will notify you and help you to bring them back down to a safe level. You will be back to painting safely in no time!

How it Works

PaintSafe uses a CSS811 Air Quality Sensor and a TMP006 Temperature Sensor to accurately measure the level of VOCs within your workspace every 5 seconds. Using a microcomputer, PaintSafe recieves your sensor's data and compares it to a safe threshold level. This level is calculated based on information given by you, the user.

How it Ensures Your Safety

PaintSafe works to ensure a healthy working environment for you in a number of ways, depending on your preferences! On the most primitive level, it will send you notifications about recommended actions to take in order to get rid of the toxic VOCs. On a more advanced level, Paintsafe can interact with your smart-home appliances; such as smart windows and ventilation systems, doing the work for you.

Attach your sensor to your clothes via the slidable clip at the back or via a carabiner through the clip hole!

Some Interesting Facts

VOC Levels

VOC levels can be super concentrated in indoor spaces - up to ten times higher than outdoors!

Common Sources

Household products are the main emitters of indoor VOCs with paints, varnishes and cleaning products being some of the main culprits.

Health Effects

Exposure to high concentrations of VOCs can lead to numerous health problems ranging from eye iratation, headaches, liver damage and even cancer!

Waiting For Paint To Dry?

Because they're volatile, these compounds vaporize and emit gasses, even long after they've dried. Paint emits only half of its VOCs in the first year.
